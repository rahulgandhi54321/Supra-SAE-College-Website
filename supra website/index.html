                                 About Team

  
Team Ragnar of Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University Gandhinagar , represents a group of
race car enthusiasts, that has a sole purpose to build a high performance race
car. The team consists of students that are perfectly assigned to different
domains in order to maximize the overall efficiency of the team. This makes
team Ragnar different any other group of students.

About institute

Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University’s 100 acre campus is located in Gandhinagar, which is the capital city of Gujarat and located 23 Km North from a well developed city called Ahmedabad with a population of 8 million people. The city is famous for its remarkable cultural development and social life.PDPU offers multiple courses ranging from engineering, arts and management along with maximum exposure and opportunities to its students through various national and International exchange programs with Best Universities worldwide. For development of its faculties and staff the University endeavors for various Joint Exchange and Research programs.
